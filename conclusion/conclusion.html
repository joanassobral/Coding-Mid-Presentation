Conclusion

As mentioned in the first part of this presentation, I've gone back to my first impressions of the book to reshape the narrative of this project. In the book, "bad" quality images are presented and designed to look precious, respectable, and beautiful, similarly to how it expresses the importance of things that might be seen as trivial, like childhood dreams and football.

Focusing on the nature of the images of the book, I want to experiment with ways an image can look "bad" quality on the web, and to subversing the expectations of how these images should be treated like the book does.
