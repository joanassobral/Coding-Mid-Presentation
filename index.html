I’m Joana, the book I’m translating into the web is No Mundo Maravilhoso do Futebol

It is a photography book about the role of football in the lives of the children of Cascalho, a favela in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. Through a series of photographs, paintings, and texts, this book beautifully portrays the presence of football as an inspiration for a better life to the members of this community.

This presentation focuses on my experience of this project so far, divided into three parts: Narrative, Coding, and Feedback. Use the titles at the top of the page to navigate.
